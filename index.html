Research

What I study

EEG and brain dynamics

I use electrophysiological signals to study neural activity related to perception, cognition, emotion regulation, cognitive load and fatigue.

Machine learning for neuroscience

I develop and evaluate computational pipelines for EEG-based classification, prediction and interpretability, including applications to affective computing.

Digital health and human states

I am interested in combining neural, physiological and behavioural signals to model wellbeing, workload and adaptive human-computer interaction.
